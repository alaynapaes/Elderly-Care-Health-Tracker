Daily Care Tips

  • Ensure medicines are taken on time every day without skipping doses.
  • Monitor vital signs regularly such as blood pressure, sugar, and heart rate.
  • Keep the patient well hydrated throughout the day.
  • Encourage light physical activity like walking or stretching if possible.
  • Maintain a clean and safe environment to prevent falls.
  • Ensure proper sleep and rest for recovery and stability.
  • Provide emotional support and regular social interaction.

Warning Signs

  • Blood Pressure above 160 or below 90
  • Sugar level below 60 or above 250
  • Oxygen level below 92%
  • High fever above 100.4°F (38°C)
  • Sudden confusion, dizziness, or unconsciousness
  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Severe weakness or inability to move

Seek immediate medical help if any of these symptoms are observed.
